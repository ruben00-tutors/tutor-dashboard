Dashboard Glossary

Definitions of every metric, column, and term used in this dashboard.

Positioning metrics

Position (May 2026)
Positioning
The current organic search ranking of the tutor listing page on VosCours.fr for its target keyword. Position 1 is the top result — lower numbers are always better. Listings with position 0 are excluded from this dashboard as they have no measurable ranking.
A listing at position 5 appears on the first page of Google. Position 30+ is typically on page 3 or beyond — very low visibility.
Excluded (position 0)
Positioning
Listings showing position 0 have been removed from the analysis. This typically means the page is not yet indexed by Google, has no measurable ranking, or the listing does not exist as a standalone page.

Acquisition metrics

Active tutors
Acquisition
The total number of active tutors currently registered and visible on the listing page. More tutors enrich the page content — the core lever of this campaign.
Progress (acquired/target)
Acquisition
New tutor conversions achieved since the campaign started, shown as "acquired / target" with a percentage. A dark blue bar indicates the listing has exceeded its target (≥100%).
101/85 (119%) means the listing exceeded its target — e.g. Piano national. 4/40 (10%) means it is far behind.
Over target
Acquisition
Listings where progress has exceeded the acquisition target (progress ≥ target). These are highlighted in the filter and the progress bar turns dark blue.

Demand metrics

L/T Ratio Leads ÷ Tutors
Demand
The ratio of active student leads to active tutors on a listing. A high ratio signals strong unmet demand — these listings are highest priority for acquisition.
Accordéon Paris ville: L/T = 20.0 → 20 students per 1 tutor = critical shortage. Cuisine: L/T = 0.2 → very low demand relative to tutor supply.

Listing structure

Location: "–"
Structure
National-level listing for the subject, aggregating all tutors across France. Has its own independent search ranking.
Location: city name
Structure
City-level listing ranking for local queries (e.g. "cours de tennis à Paris"). Tracked independently from the national page.
Status: In Progress
Structure
Tutor acquisition campaign is active for this listing.
Status: Done
Structure
Acquisition target reached — campaign complete.
Status: Not Started
Structure
Listing identified as priority but acquisition has not yet begun.
